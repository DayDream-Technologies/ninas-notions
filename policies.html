Shipping Policy

We ship Monday through Friday, excluding holidays. Orders placed before 2:00 PM EST typically ship the same day.

Shipping Options

  • Standard Shipping (5-7 business days): $5.99 for orders under $50, FREE for orders $50+
  • Priority Shipping (2-3 business days): $9.99
  • Express Shipping (1-2 business days): $14.99

Local Pickup

Free in-store pickup is available! Select "Local Pickup" at checkout and we'll notify you when your order is ready. Orders are typically ready within 2-4 hours during store hours.

Shipping Restrictions

We currently ship within the continental United States. For Alaska, Hawaii, and international orders, please contact us for shipping quotes.

Returns & Exchanges

Your satisfaction is our priority! We want you to be happy with your purchase.

Return Policy

  • Returns accepted within 30 days of purchase
  • Items must be in original, unopened packaging
  • Receipt or proof of purchase required
  • Refunds issued to original payment method

Non-Returnable Items

  • Opened paper packs and adhesive products
  • Class registrations (may be transferred with 48-hour notice)
  • Gift certificates
  • Sale or clearance items marked "Final Sale"

Exchanges

We're happy to exchange items for store credit or a different product. Bring your item and receipt to the store, or contact us to arrange a mail-in exchange.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no additional cost.

Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

In-Store Payments

  • Cash
  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
  • Nina's Notions Gift Certificates
  • Personal Checks (with valid ID)

Online Payments

  • Credit/Debit Cards
  • PayPal
  • Nina's Notions Gift Certificates

All online transactions are secured with SSL encryption to protect your information.
